What You Need to Know

NeoTechVault is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program and the eBay Partner Network, affiliate advertising programs designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com and eBay.com.

How We Earn

When you purchase products through our affiliate links, we receive a commission from Amazon or eBay. The commission percentage varies by product category but typically ranges from 1-10% of the purchase price.

Important: You do NOT pay any extra fees or charges because you clicked through our links. The price you pay is exactly the same as if you went directly to Amazon or eBay.
